What Can Uber Learning Games Provide?

We currently have three games that can bring your Literacy Skills to the next level

We have created our games to provide our students with the perfect balance of being enjoyable and educational.

By receiving feedback from former students, we have learned that many used the skills learned from our games to then being used in Higher-level Education.

Twisty Perception

Listening Game

Twisty Perception is a listening game where students will be asked to put their spelling and listening skills together. The user must be enter the word that they hear.

Age Range

This game has three difficulty settings ranging from the age of six to seventeen, giving the user a wide range of ways to test their skills with this game.

Skills Gained

Students will gain literacy and listening skills when playing this game, students who believe they can spell well will be surprised when asked to spell words played via audio.

Popularity

With over three age difficulties, numerous audio tracks to listen to, this game is our most popular yet when it comes students who are interested in a challenge they can simply change age group.

Sherlock Words

Missing Words Game

Sherlock Words is a missing words game where users are asked to choose between three answers, with only one having the correct spelling to match the sentence.

Age Range

This game is recommended for our younger students as well as other students who maybe having problems with their spelling skill and need practice to improve it.

Skills Gained

Students will gain spelling skills when playing this game, they will learn correct word spellings as well as learning how to make sure a word is spelt correctly.

Popularity

This game is mostly popular with our younger students as the game itself is fairly easy with students been given images of what the correct word is.

Library Brains

Definition Game

Library Brains is a definition game where users are asked to decide what word out of three choices matches the correct definition.

Age Range

This game has is suitable for older students that have already played our other games and feel that their spelling skills are advanced enough to play Library Brains.

Skills Gained

Students will gain literacy and as well as definitions of words that they may not of known before, definitions we have chosen are very popular and needed for the future.

Popularity

This is one of our most popular games, with it being suited for more advanced students as well as older students who are interested in learning more about their language..
